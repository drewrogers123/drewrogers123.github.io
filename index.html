About Me

I enjoy tackling problems by building practical tools and turning ideas into working solutions. I have a Bachelors degree in theoretical physics and did graduate work studying mathematical physics. My past research spanned gauge theory, dark matter, cosmology, general relativity, and chaos theory.

These projects reflect how I approach challenges: breaking them down, designing efficient solutions, and applying both analytical and creative thinking. I'm continually learning, improving my technical toolkit, and looking for opportunities to apply these skills to real-world problems in impactful ways.
